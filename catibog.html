My Journey as a BSIT Student

Hi! I’m a BS Information Technology student at Cavite State University (CvSU), and this blog is where I share my journey into the world of web development. It all started with a simple curiosity: How are websites made? That question led me down a path of endless discovery. As I explored the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I realized how powerful these tools are in building the digital world around us.

Right now, I’m trying to build multiple projects—ranging from static pages to interactive, mobile-responsive websites. I’m learning to design clean layouts, code user-friendly interfaces, and debug problems (a lot of them!). Every challenge taught me something new—not just about tech, but about patience, creativity, and persistence.

Along the way, I’ve also started exploring tools and frameworks like Bootstrap, Tailwind CSS, and version control with Git. I love seeing how small lines of code can bring big ideas to life.

This blog is a reflection of my growth—not just in skills, but in mindset. Whether you're a fellow student, aspiring developer, or just someone curious about tech, I hope my posts inspire or help you in some way. I'm still learning, still experimenting, and still building—and I'm excited for what’s ahead. Thanks for being part of the journey!
